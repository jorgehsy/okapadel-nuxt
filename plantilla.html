######## PISTAS BASIC

Our
Basic Court

model is very versatile. This court features pillars every two meters. Its classic yet elegant design guarantees maximum robustness and complies with all quality regulations and stand-ards.

Versatile padel courts that are easy to install and maintain.

Technical Specifications

The Basic Court model is a very complete court featuring a continuous side mesh, an anti-vibration system for its structure, and reinforced brackets at all joints. All Basic Courts have been designed by our in-house engineers; thus, we are thoroughly aware of the specifica-tions and can answer any questions you may have.

A simple and safe way to build an indoor padel court.

Metal Structure

  • 3000x2000 mm side modules made with 40x20x1.5 mm metal sections and 50x50x4 electro-welded mesh.
  • Modules for glass panes measuring 3000x2000 mm with 30x30x1.5 mm metal sec-tions.
  • 4000x2000 mm end modules made with 30x30x1.5 mm metal sections and 50x50x4 electro-welded mesh.
  • All metal sections are made of S235JR galvanized steel in accordance with UNE-EN 10025, anchoring bases are made of S275JO hot-rolled sheet metal in accordance with UNE-EN10204/2.2.
  • Pillars measuring 3 m and 4 m in height and featuring 80x60x2 mm galvanized steel sections with 200x200x10 mm anchoring bases.
  • Pilares dobles de las esquinas de perfil 80x60x2 mm con la base de anclaje de 300x300x10mm;
  • Double corner pillars with 80x60x2 mm galvanized steel sections and 300x300x10 mm anchoring bases.
  • Lighting pillars measuring 6 m in height and featuring 80x60x2 mm galvanized steel sections and 200x200x10 mm anchoring bases – as well as 12 mm thick reinforce-ment brackets.

Anchoring System

At Okapadel we know that the playing field makes all the difference in a competitive court. For this reason, the Basic Court is anchored to the ground with M16x100 mechanical an-chors. The modules are attached to the pillars with JT2-6-5.5x22-V16 self-drilling screws and the joints between modules are secured with DIN 931 screws and DIN 985 self-locking nuts.

Anchors and fasteners for our Basic Court model: easy installation and maintenance with total security and all possible guarantees.

TemperedGlass

Our Basic Courts are equipped with tempered safety glass enclosures in accordance with UNE-EN 12150-1:2016+A1:2020. There are 18 panes measuring 3m high by 2m wide and 10 mm thick. They feature polished edges and countersunk holes. Our glass panes are com-pletely smoothed over for flushness to prevent the ball from deflecting unpredictably and inju-ries to players.

Glass panes for padel courts – glass padel courts.

Paint

The metal structure of our Basic Court is covered with an Epoxi/Poliéster powder coating for exteriors polymerized at 200ºC.

The characteristics of this paint are as follows:

  • Excellent adhesion and flexibility, even allowing folding.
  • Great resistance to corrosion, heat, impact, abrasion, weathering, and extreme tem-perature changes.
  • Hardness and durability when faced with impact, torsion, and slow deformation.
  • Resistance to extreme weather conditions such as the effects of the sun, ultraviolet rays, heat, cold, and humidity.
  • Friendly with the environment. The paint is manufactured and applied without the use of solvents, toxic gases are not emitted, and the manufacture process produces a very low environmental impact.

Various color options are available.

Ecological, zero-VOC paint for padel courts.

Grass

The playing field for our Basic Court is covered with artificial sports turf. This grass is re-sistant to UV rays because of its composition and its high decitex values. Fibers measure 10-15 mm in height. Rounded quartz sand, washed and dried with 97% SiO2, grain size be-tween 0.5 and 0.8 mm.

Color a consultar.

Artificial padel court turf.

Lightning

Our Basic Courts come with a comprehensive lighting system for playing on low-light days or at night. The lighting pillars feature four 200W LED lights – 24,000 lm each. They offer high performance with minimal power consumption. High quality and excellent durability (average life: 5000 h).

Padel court spotlights - LED lighting for padel courts.

Customization

We can print on anything from the pavilion's canvas to the court floor with any design you want. We have all the solutions for your personalized padel tent. For signage in our Basic Court models, we use digital technology featuring sublimation printing on the canvas cano-pies, yielding a vibrant and very durable result.
